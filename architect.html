Cybersecurity & UAV Systems Security

I am a Cybersecurity student with a strong interest in system security, network protocols, and embedded systems, particularly in the context of UAVs (drones). I prefer a practical, system-level approach, focusing on how components interact in real environments rather than isolated theory.

My focus is on understanding how drone systems operate end-to-end, including flight controllers, communication protocols like MAVLink, and potential security vulnerabilities in these systems. I am interested in securing real-world autonomous systems by combining cybersecurity principles with embedded and aerial platforms.
